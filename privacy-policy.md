# Privacy Policy for UPRISE

**Last Updated: February 13, 2026**

## Introduction

Welcome to UPRISE ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience when using our mobile application.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our UPRISE mobile application (the "App"). Please read this privacy policy carefully.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
When you create an account, we collect:
- Email address
- Password (encrypted and never stored in plain text)
- Account creation date

### Health & Wellness Data
As a mental wellness app, we collect the following sensitive data that you voluntarily provide:
- **Mood logs** — daily mood ratings (1–5 scale) and mood entries you record
- **Habit completion data** — which wellness habits you completed and when
- **Streak information** — how many consecutive days you have completed habits
- **Achievement data** — milestones and badges you have earned
- **Weekly wellness reports** — aggregated mood and habit data we generate for you

This data is used solely to provide you with your wellness insights and is never sold or shared with third parties for advertising purposes.

### Usage Data
We automatically collect:
- Alarm times and settings
- Mission completion records (Photo, Math, Memory, Gratitude, Shake, Squat)
- App usage statistics
- Device type and operating system

### Information We Do NOT Collect
- We do not access your contacts
- We do not access your photos except when you voluntarily take a photo to complete a Photo alarm mission — these photos are not stored on our servers
- We do not track your location
- We do not collect payment information directly (handled by Apple App Store)
- We do not share your mood or health data with advertisers, employers, or insurers

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide and maintain the App functionality
- Send alarm notifications and streak at-risk reminders
- Display your mood trends and wellness insights in Weekly Reports
- Track your habits, streaks, and achievements
- Improve app performance and user experience
- Communicate with you about updates and features
- Process your subscription (Premium features)
- Provide customer support at support@myuprise.app

## Mood & Mental Health Data

We take your mental health data seriously. Specifically:

- **Your mood data is private by default.** It is only visible to you.
- **We do not use your mood data for advertising.**
- **We do not sell your mood or health data** to any third party.
- **We do not share your mood data** with employers, insurance companies, or government agencies.
- Your mood data is stored securely and encrypted on Supabase servers.
- You can delete all your mood data at any time by deleting your account.

## Notifications

We send the following types of push notifications:
- **Alarm notifications** — at the times you schedule your alarms
- **Streak at-risk reminders** — at 8:00 PM if you have a habit streak of 3+ days and have not completed that habit today
- **Achievement notifications** — when you unlock a new badge milestone
- **App update notifications** — important product announcements (infrequent)

You can disable all notifications at any time in your device settings or in Settings → App Settings → Notifications inside UPRISE.

## Data Storage and Security

### Where We Store Data
Your data is stored securely on Supabase (our database provider) servers located in the United States.

### Security Measures
We implement industry-standard security measures including:
- Encryption of passwords (never stored in plain text)
- Secure HTTPS connections for all data transmission
- Row-level security policies in our database
- Access controls and authentication via Supabase Auth
- Regular security updates

### Data Retention
We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may delete your account at any time, which will remove all your personal data including mood logs, habit data, and achievements from our servers within 30 days.

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

### Supabase
We use Supabase for:
- User authentication
- Database storage (habits, mood logs, achievements, completions)
- Real-time data synchronization

Supabase Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

### Apple App Store
For payment processing and app distribution. All subscription payments are handled by Apple.

Apple Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

Expo Privacy Policy: https://expo.dev/privacy

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access** your personal data — contact us and we will provide an export
- **Correct** inaccurate data — update via the app or contact support
- **Delete** your account and all associated data (including mood logs)
- **Export** your data — contact support@myuprise.app
- **Opt-out** of marketing communications — unsubscribe link in any email
- **Withdraw consent** at any time by deleting your account

## Children's Privacy

UPRISE is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at support@myuprise.app and we will promptly delete it.

## California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):
- Right to know what personal information is collected
- Right to know whether personal information is sold or disclosed and to whom
- Right to delete personal information
- Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information — **we do not sell your data**
- Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

## International Users

If you are accessing the App from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States where our servers are located. By using UPRISE, you consent to this transfer.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
- Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document
- Sending you an email notification for material changes that affect how we handle your data

## Data Deletion Request

To delete your account and all associated data (including all mood logs, habit data, streaks, and achievements):
1. Open the UPRISE app
2. Go to Settings
3. Tap "Delete Account"
4. Confirm deletion

Or email us at support@myuprise.app with the subject "Delete My Account"

We will process deletion requests within 30 days.
